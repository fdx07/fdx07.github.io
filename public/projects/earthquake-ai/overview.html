Trained on the 2015 Nepal quake’s 260 601-building dataset, this three-class neural network converts raw structural and location features into an instant “low / medium / severe” damage map. The balanced model hits about 71 % overall accuracy—enough to steer first-responders toward the worst-hit blocks while the dust is still settling.
